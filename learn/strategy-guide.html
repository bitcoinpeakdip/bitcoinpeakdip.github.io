4 Core Strategies

1. RECLAIM_FAILURE

MEDIUM RISK 68% Win Rate

When to use: When price breaks below VWAP and fails to reclaim it

Entry Conditions:

  • Price breaks below VWAP with volume
  • Failed reclaim attempt (price touches VWAP but rejects)
  • RSI below 50
  • Volume confirmation on the breakdown

Best Timeframes: 5M, 15M

2. DISTRIBUTION_FADE

HIGH RISK 62% Win Rate

When to use: During clear distribution patterns at peak areas

Entry Conditions:

  • Clear distribution pattern (higher prices, lower volume)
  • Low volume rally to resistance
  • Rejection from key resistance level
  • Bearish divergence on RSI/MACD

3. HEDGE_FLIP

LOW RISK 72% Win Rate

When to use: When price flips from above to below VWAP with confirmation

Entry Conditions:

  • Price crosses below VWAP
  • Confirmed close below VWAP
  • Volume spike on the flip
  • Multiple timeframe confirmation

4. CONTINUATION

MEDIUM RISK 75% Win Rate

When to use: During established downtrends after pullbacks

Entry Conditions:

  • Established downtrend (lower highs, lower lows)
  • Pullback to resistance/trendline
  • Bearish continuation pattern (flag, pennant)
  • Volume confirmation on breakdown
